Why AMI matters

         

Diagnostic delays and reporting variability are critical bottlenecks for patient outcomes. AMI’s multimodal fusion dramatically reduces time-to-insight by combining image analysis with laboratory interpretation and clinical context — producing a single, prioritized report that clinicians can act on within minutes.

         
           
             

Clinician-centric reports

             
Concise findings, ranked differential, evidence links to image regions and lab anomalies.
           
           
             

Built for hospitals

             
Integrates with PACS and LIS, supports batch intake and audit logging for workflows.

Technology

     

AMI is a layered platform combining validated image networks, clinical NLP, and lab-pattern models. The fusion layer aligns multimodal features to produce a single, interpretable diagnostic view.

     
       
Explainable AI outputs (saliency & rule traces)
       
Federated-ready model training
       
PACS & LIS connectors (HL7, DICOM)
       
Clinical NLP & ontology mapping (SNOMED/LOINC)
     
     
       

Clinical validation & safety

       

Models are validated on curated retrospective datasets and continuously monitored in production with human-in-the-loop review. Drift detection and performance dashboards ensure clinical safety and auditability.
